How do I use BackTalk?




Using BackTalk is as easy as 1, 2, 3:

1) Record your speech on the "Record" page. Once you are finished, hit the stop button.

2) Check your scores. The system will keep records of the last 30 practice sessions on the "Past Results" page.

3) Explore the details. Use the button bar at the top of the "Past Results" page to see your performance over time in the different trouble spots of public speaking.
